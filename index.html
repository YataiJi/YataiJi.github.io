Biography

I am now a master student (year 2) from IIG group in Tsinghua University, supervised by Prof. Yujiu Yang. I received my bachelor degree in Department of Automation from Tsinghua University in 2021. My major research interests are MultiModal Learning. Recently, I focus on VLP(Vision Language Pre-training) and some downstream tasks, such as VQA(Visual Question Answering) and VTG(Video Temporal Grounding).
